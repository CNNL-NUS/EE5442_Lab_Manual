Sentaurus TCAD Introduction

  • Technology Computer-Aided Design (TCAD) refers to using computer simulations to develop and optimize semiconductor processing technologies and devices.
  • TCAD simulation tools solve fundamental, physical, partial differential equations, such as diffusion and transport equations for discretized geometries.
  • TCAD simulations are used widely in the semiconductor industry.
